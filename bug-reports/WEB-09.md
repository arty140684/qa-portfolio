# WEB-09

## Title
"More" links in Help section do not open pages for SCA license

## Environment
- Website: capital.com
- License: SCA
- Browser: Chrome

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open capital.com
2. Select SCA license
3. Go to Help section
4. Click "More" in any category

## Expected Result
Relevant help article page opens

## Actual Result
Page does not open or is inaccessible

## Impact
- Users cannot access help content
- Blocks access to important support information
- Affects onboarding and problem resolution

## Notes
Issue may be related to missing pages for SCA license or incorrect routing.
