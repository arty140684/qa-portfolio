# WEB-10

## Title
Privacy Policy link is not clickable in Sign Up form (Arabic language)

## Environment
- Website: capital.com
- License: SCA
- Language: AR
- Browser: Chrome

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open capital.com
2. Select SCA license
3. Switch to Arabic language
4. Open Sign Up form
5. Try to click Privacy Policy link

## Expected Result
Privacy Policy page opens

## Actual Result
Link is not clickable

## Impact
- Users cannot access legal information
- May violate compliance requirements
- Reduces trust in platform

## Notes
Issue appears only in Arabic localization.
