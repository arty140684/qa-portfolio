# WEB-08

## Title
Instagram link in Social media block redirects to login page instead of company profile

## Environment
- Website: capital.com
- License: SCA (UAE)
- Language: EN
- Browser: Chrome

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open Help page
2. Click "More" in any section
3. Scroll to Social media
4. Click Instagram

## Expected Result
Official company Instagram page is opened

## Actual Result
User is redirected to:
https://www.instagram.com/accounts/login/?next=...

## Impact
- Users cannot access company social media
- Reduces trust and brand credibility
- Breaks external navigation

## Notes
Incorrect URL used for Instagram link.
Other social links may need verification.
